# Graphic and abstract style cards

Use these cards when the image is itself a designed surface or symbolic form. Keep hierarchy stronger than decoration.

## Deconstructed editorial collage

- `id`: `deconstructed-editorial-collage`
- `name_en`: Deconstructed Editorial Collage
- `name_zh`: 解构编辑拼贴
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Magazine covers, cultural portraits, fashion editorials, identity campaigns and concept-led profile art.
- `not_for`: Clean ecommerce, documentary portraits, children's illustration, literal diagrams.
- `visual_dna`: Recognizable portrait interrupted by tactile paper planes, architectural grids or dissolving blocks; analog tear edges meet precise editorial geometry; a restrained neutral field keeps fragmentation legible.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Tight portrait or three-figure horizontal; subject remains the anchor; layers cross but do not erase eyes and gesture; soft editorial light; ivory, charcoal, ochre, muted olive and one accent; torn paper, pencil construction, printed photo and matte plaster fragments.
- `boundaries`: Use two collage mechanisms maximum. Preserve identity and silhouette. Give fragments a directional logic. Typography is a separate overlay zone, not random microtext.
- `template`: `A premium deconstructed editorial collage of [SUBJECT], identity and expression clearly preserved while [TORN PAPER PLANES / ARCHITECTURAL GRID / DISSOLVING BLOCKS] pass through selected edges, layered printed photography, matte paper fibers, pencil construction lines and restrained geometric marks on a warm neutral field, soft editorial lighting, palette of ivory charcoal ochre muted olive and one [ACCENT], deliberate asymmetry and clear negative space, no invented text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Covering both eyes; every collage effect at once; random stickers; scrapbook clutter; saturated rainbow pieces; broken anatomy mistaken for collage.

## Psychedelic abstract cover

- `id`: `psychedelic-abstract-cover`
- `name_en`: Psychedelic Abstract Cover
- `name_zh`: 迷幻抽象封面
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Album art, festival covers, expressive wallpapers, music visualization and energetic identity fields.
- `not_for`: Corporate reports, restrained luxury, precise diagrams, narrative character art.
- `visual_dna`: One continuous force field—vortex, glossy flow or radial mandala—rather than scattered shapes; high chroma held together by a dominant motion; tactile paint or polished resin; image works without text.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Square/vertical cover; central vortex, radial symmetry or sweeping S-curve; edge-to-edge image; three dominant hues plus a small metallic/light accent; oil impasto, viscous glossy resin or fine fractal filigree.
- `boundaries`: Choose exactly one motion grammar and one material. Maintain a calm focal center or clean title zone. Keep black/neutral separation so color does not become visual noise.
- `template`: `An abstract psychedelic cover driven by one [VORTEX / RADIAL MANDALA / FLOWING S-CURVE], edge-to-edge [OIL IMPASTO / ULTRA-GLOSSY RESIN / FINE FRACTAL FILIGREE], dominant palette of [COLOR 1], [COLOR 2] and [COLOR 3] with restrained [METALLIC / LIGHT] accents, strong directional rhythm, deep color separation, one calm focal center or reserved title zone, square album-art composition, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Unrelated floating blobs; every rainbow hue at equal weight; no focal rhythm; prompt mixing oil, glass and watercolor; decorative AI noise; automatic fake album title.

## Faceted metallic neo-cubism

- `id`: `faceted-metallic-neo-cubism`
- `name_en`: Faceted Metallic Neo-Cubism
- `name_zh`: 切面金属新立体主义
- `validation_status`: experimental-corpus-reviewed / generation-pending (two-source cluster)
- `trigger`: Futuristic profile art, technology campaign objects, music visuals, symbolic animals and sculptural brand imagery.
- `not_for`: Soft beauty portraits, realistic wildlife, architecture documentation, friendly children's characters.
- `visual_dna`: Subject reconstructed from interlocking angular planes; profile or motion silhouette remains instantly recognizable; dark void isolates the sculpture; iridescent metal catches cyan, violet, magenta and amber edge light.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Centered floating bust/object or dynamic full-body animal; three-quarter/profile view; hard rim lights from opposing hues; deep purple-blue gradient void; brushed and anodized metal with crisp bevels; minimal ground or none.
- `boundaries`: Preserve the subject's primary silhouette. Use large intentional facets, not low-poly noise. Keep one spiral/curve counterform at most. Materials stay metallic rather than plastic.
- `template`: `A sculptural neo-cubist interpretation of [SUBJECT], recognizable [PROFILE / ACTION] rebuilt from large interlocking metallic planes and sharp geometric wedges, floating against a dark purple-blue gradient void, brushed and anodized metal with crisp bevels, opposing cyan-violet and amber-magenta rim lights, dramatic three-quarter composition, a few controlled curved counterforms, premium 3D studio render, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Uniform tiny triangles; melted chrome; rainbow plastic; lost silhouette; busy environment; low-poly game asset look.
