# Illustration and storytelling style cards

Use one card as a visual grammar, then replace only the bracketed subject and scene slots. Do not mix two cards unless the user explicitly requests a hybrid.

## Cozy watercolor storybook

- `id`: `cozy-watercolor-storybook`
- `name_en`: Cozy Watercolor Storybook
- `name_zh`: 温暖水彩绘本
- `validation_status`: experimental-corpus-reviewed / generation-pending (single-source cluster)
- `trigger`: Cozy children's book scenes, gentle family stories, cottages, gardens, animal friendships, nostalgic everyday adventures.
- `not_for`: Hard sci-fi, horror, glossy advertising, photorealism, action scenes that need sharp impact.
- `visual_dna`: Hand-inked contours with uneven pressure; transparent watercolor washes; tiny story-carrying props; rounded, friendly silhouettes; tactile off-white paper; calm, humane observation.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Wide establishing view or readable medium-wide tableau; foreground plants frame a clear path to the subject; soft daylight; sky blue, moss green, wheat, terracotta and muted flower accents; watercolor bloom, pencil hatching and visible paper grain.
- `boundaries`: Keep one main action and one secondary discovery. Preserve breathing room. Faces stay simple and expressive. Texture must read as physical media, never as glossy 3D.
- `template`: `A cozy hand-illustrated children's picture-book scene of [SUBJECT] [ACTION] in [PLACE], one clear visual story with small discoverable details, friendly rounded shapes, delicate ink contours, transparent watercolor and colored-pencil texture on warm off-white paper, soft natural daylight, muted sky-blue moss-green wheat and terracotta palette, wide readable composition, gentle nostalgic mood, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Generic anime eyes; hyper-detailed pores; oversaturated rainbow palette; flat vector fills; empty wallpaper composition; random decorative clutter with no story function.
- `references`:
  - `tweet_id: 2072542433213391291`; `image_id: 20446`; `image: images/2072542433213391291/1.jpg`

## Cinematic anime night city

- `id`: `cinematic-anime-night-city`
- `name_en`: Cinematic Anime Night City
- `name_zh`: 电影感动漫夜城
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Rainy city stories, quiet magical encounters, retro-future streets, melancholic urban characters, anime establishing shots.
- `not_for`: Flat character sheets, cheerful kawaii icons, daylight product art, text-heavy posters.
- `visual_dna`: Drawn anime characters embedded in a richly observed city; wet asphalt and practical signage; cool ambient blue opposed by warm windows or neon; atmospheric depth; a single narrative anomaly or emotional anchor.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Street-level wide or medium-wide frame; converging streets pull the eye inward; foreground subject occupies less than half the frame; rain, bloom and reflected light; inked animation contours over painterly backgrounds; blue-violet base with amber, cyan or magenta accents.
- `boundaries`: The environment must carry at least half the story. Use one hero color contrast. Signage is atmospheric shape, not legible copy. Keep anatomy and perspective grounded.
- `template`: `A cinematic anime film still of [CHARACTER] [ACTION] on [CITY STREET] at night, richly observed architecture and shopfronts, wet pavement reflections, cool blue-hour ambient light contrasted with warm practical windows and restrained [ACCENT COLOR] neon, atmospheric rain or mist, painterly background with clean 2D character linework, street-level [SHOT], deep environmental storytelling, one subtle magical or emotional focal point, no readable text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Character floating over a generic blurred background; every surface glowing neon; random Japanese text; excessive lens flare; game UI; chibi proportions.
- `references`:
  - `tweet_id: 2034533564293767647`; `image_id: 1629`; `image: images/2034533564293767647/1.jpg`

## Notion editorial line art

- `id`: `notion-editorial-line-art`
- `name_en`: Notion Editorial Line Art
- `name_zh`: Notion 式编辑线稿
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Product explainers, onboarding illustrations, blog headers, lightweight workplace stories, friendly avatars and concept metaphors.
- `not_for`: Dramatic realism, luxury campaigns, painterly landscapes, detailed technical diagrams.
- `visual_dna`: Crisp black monoline with slight hand-drawn warmth; simplified anatomy; very sparse fills; white field as an active design element; one readable gesture; editorial restraint.
- `composition_light_color_material`: One full-body or medium-shot subject; large negative space; orthographic or eye-level view; no modeled lighting; black and white with at most one muted accent; clean vector-like stroke on white.
- `boundaries`: Preserve silhouette clarity at thumbnail size. Limit props to what explains the action. Avoid cross-hatching and gradients. Use diversity through pose and clothing, not facial detail.
- `template`: `A minimalist editorial line-art illustration of [SUBJECT] [ACTION], one instantly readable gesture, simplified friendly anatomy, crisp slightly imperfect black monoline, sparse black fills, pure white background with generous negative space, eye-level flat composition, at most one muted [ACCENT COLOR] detail, modern knowledge-product aesthetic, no shadows, no gradients, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Dense scenery; photoreal face; thick comic outlines; emoji-like icons; random decoration; multiple competing actions.

## Handcrafted clay character

- `id`: `handcrafted-clay-character`
- `name_en`: Handcrafted Clay Character
- `name_zh`: 手作黏土角色
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `pilot_note`: A directional welcome-page trial favored this card's tactile material rules, but the card remains generation-pending.
- `trigger`: Cute mascots, stop-motion characters, educational animals, tactile collectible figures, gentle miniature storytelling.
- `not_for`: Fast action, polished metal products, realistic humans, large environments.
- `visual_dna`: Finger-shaped matte volume; deliberately simplified proportions; oversized eyes or one strong facial cue; tiny tool marks; physically plausible contact and weight; warm handmade charm.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Centered full-body figure or small group; eye-level or slight high angle; large softbox light; shallow depth of field only when an environment exists; clay, ceramic or felt-like surface; restrained earthy/pastel palette.
- `boundaries`: Choose one primary handmade material and keep it consistent. Preserve small imperfections without making the object dirty. Background stays simple enough to reveal silhouette.
- `template`: `A handcrafted stop-motion-style [SUBJECT] made from matte kneaded clay, [POSE OR ACTION], simplified rounded proportions, expressive oversized [FACIAL FEATURE], visible subtle fingerprints and sculpting marks, physically believable weight and contact, centered full-body composition, soft diffused studio light, restrained [EARTHY OR PASTEL] palette, simple tactile backdrop, charming but not childish, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Perfect glossy plastic; mixed materials on every body part; floating feet; hyperreal fur; crowded toy shelf; generic Pixar imitation.
