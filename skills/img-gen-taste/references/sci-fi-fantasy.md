# Sci-fi, fantasy and game style cards

Build a world from one governing physical rule. Scale, atmosphere and traversal should explain that rule before decorative lore does.

## Dark techno-mythic cathedral

- `id`: `dark-techno-mythic-cathedral`
- `name_en`: Dark Techno-Mythic Cathedral
- `name_zh`: 黑暗科技神话圣殿
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Boss arenas, dark mythology, techno-religious civilizations, game key art, monumental antagonist reveals.
- `not_for`: Cozy fantasy, clean science education, light adventure, small character portraits.
- `visual_dna`: Sacred axial architecture fused with machinery; colossal seated or haloed entities; one tiny pilgrim/hero supplies scale; repeated circles, chains, ribs and circuitry establish ritual order; metal, bone ceramic and stone feel ancient and functional.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Ultra-wide or 16:9; strict symmetry or near-symmetry; low camera; foreground lone figure, midground stairs, background deities; black iron, oxidized bronze, bone and sparse cobalt/plasma; backlight, halo light and volumetric dust.
- `boundaries`: One civilization and one ritual. Keep the scale ladder readable. Ornament must follow a repeated construction logic. Reserve the brightest light for the sacred/hostile focal point.
- `template`: `A monumental dark techno-mythic cathedral where [ANCIENT MYTH / RITUAL] is engineered through [MACHINE SYSTEM], colossal [DEITIES / MACHINE SAINTS] enthroned along a strict axial hierarchy, one tiny [HERO / PILGRIM] in the foreground for scale, low wide camera, repeated halos chains ribs and circuitry, corroded black iron oxidized bronze bone ceramic and worn stone, sparse [COBALT / RED PLASMA] light, deep shadow and volumetric dust, cinematic AAA concept art, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Random gears; every surface equally detailed; ten unrelated religions; bright rainbow magic; close-up without scale; generic gothic church with robots pasted in.

## Floating isometric game world

- `id`: `floating-isometric-game-world`
- `name_en`: Floating Isometric Game World
- `name_zh`: 悬浮等距游戏世界
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Worldbuilding thumbnails, strategy-game regions, whimsical settlements, theme-park maps and collectible dioramas.
- `not_for`: Realistic urban planning, first-person action, empty landscapes, flat icons.
- `visual_dna`: A self-contained island with a visible edge and readable biome; one oversized landmark organizes smaller functional buildings; roads and water form a traversal loop; miniature citizens imply activity; tilt-shift scale model clarity.
- `composition_light_color_material`: 30-45 degree high-angle view; island centered against sky/void; deep focus across settlement with mild miniature falloff; warm sunset or bright daylight; stone, timber, copper, water and vegetation; stylized 3D game render.
- `boundaries`: Every building needs a role. Maintain one theme. Show entrance, path network and destination. Do not hide geography under props or labels.
- `template`: `A premium stylized 3D isometric floating miniature world themed around [WORLD FUNCTION], a self-contained [ROCK / ISLAND / PLATFORM] with one oversized [LANDMARK], several smaller functional buildings, visible paths bridges water and working areas forming a readable traversal loop, tiny inhabitants for scale, 35-degree high-angle camera, deep environmental clarity with gentle tilt-shift, [GOLDEN-HOUR / BRIGHT-DAY] volumetric light, cohesive stone timber metal and vegetation materials, polished strategy-game concept art, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Random building scatter; no island edge; landmark same size as huts; unreadable maze; unrelated biomes; excessive labels embedded in image.

## Cozy retro pixel game assets

- `id`: `cozy-retro-pixel-game-assets`
- `name_en`: Cozy Retro Pixel Game Assets
- `name_zh`: 温馨复古像素游戏资产
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Farming RPG tilesets, inventory icons, cozy food sprites, village props and production-ready 2D asset sheets.
- `not_for`: Painterly concept art, photoreal food, first-person shooters, high-resolution UI mockups.
- `visual_dna`: Deliberate pixel clusters rather than a pixelation filter; 3/4 top-down or isolated icon view; compact warm palette; bright edge highlights; consistent outline weight; each asset fits one tile logic.
- `composition_light_color_material`: White/transparent-looking sheet with aligned rows for assets, or one centered icon; no smooth gradients; light from upper left; warm brown, cream, leaf green and fruit accents; 16/32-bit readable resolution.
- `boundaries`: Declare a base tile size and perspective once. Keep scale and light direction identical. Separate sprite, shadow and background. Use no anti-aliasing.
- `template`: `A cohesive cozy retro pixel-art [ASSET SHEET / SINGLE ICON] for a top-down farming RPG, [ASSET LIST], consistent [32x32 / 48x48] tile logic and three-quarter view, deliberate pixel clusters, crisp one-pixel dark outlines, upper-left lighting, compact warm palette of cream wood-brown leaf-green and [ACCENT], simple contact shadows, evenly spaced on a clean white background, no anti-aliasing, no smooth gradients, no text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Downscaled smooth illustration; mixed perspectives; random pixel sizes; airbrushed shading; outline thickness changing per object; overlapping assets.

## Cyberpunk rain-noir city

- `id`: `cyberpunk-rain-noir-city`
- `name_en`: Cyberpunk Rain-Noir City
- `name_zh`: 赛博朋克雨夜黑色都市
- `validation_status`: corpus-reviewed / generation-pending
- `trigger`: Futuristic alleys, dystopian city posters, noir characters, neon markets, urban sci-fi key art.
- `not_for`: Clean utopia, daylight solarpunk, minimal technology diagrams, product-only renders.
- `visual_dna`: A believable dense city first, neon second; layered street, elevated transit and tower strata; rain turns signage into reflections; one human silhouette or traffic stream supplies narrative scale; cyan/red or cyan/magenta opposition.
- `composition_light_color_material`: Street-level wide or vertical low angle; wet road occupies meaningful foreground; deep perspective; low-key night, rain and volumetric haze; dark concrete, glass, cables and restrained emissive signage.
- `boundaries`: Limit neon to two dominant hues plus practical white. Preserve dark surfaces. Make signage blocks plausible but not readable. Include one path the eye can follow.
- `template`: `A cinematic cyberpunk rain-noir scene in [CITY TYPE], street-level [WIDE / VERTICAL LOW-ANGLE] view with a clear path into dense layered architecture, wet asphalt occupying the foreground and reflecting restrained [CYAN + RED / CYAN + MAGENTA] signage, elevated transit and distant towers fading into rain haze, one [LONE FIGURE / TRAFFIC STREAM] for scale, dark concrete glass and cable detail, low-key volumetric light, believable lived-in city, no readable text, no logo.`
- `anti_patterns`: Neon on every edge; dry street; floating holograms with no infrastructure; empty generic skyline; illegible text wall; crushed blacks with no material detail.
