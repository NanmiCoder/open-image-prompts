# Data licensing and attribution

## Project-authored materials

To the extent the project maintainer owns the relevant rights, the database schema, `oip-visual-v2` taxonomy, normalized labels, translations, and other project-authored metadata are made available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license.

## Third-party source content

Source prompts, posts, creator handles, source URLs, and source images remain the property of their respective creators or rights holders. They are not relicensed under MIT or CC BY 4.0 by this repository.

Each record retains provenance so users can inspect the source and comply with the source platform's terms and applicable law. Availability in this archive does not imply endorsement, transfer of ownership, or permission for commercial reuse.

## Removal requests

Rights holders may open a GitHub issue identifying the source URL or stable record ID and the requested action. Valid removal requests should be applied to the image file, DB record, downloadable archives, and future dataset versions together.
